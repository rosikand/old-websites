**Shifting the School Day Start Time**
Rohan Sikand
January 16th, 2020
The idea of shifting the school day over towards a later start time is something that deserves significiantly more attention. This is something that should happen immediately and without hesitation. We are greatly harming developing children in their ability to have a well rested sleep. Sleep is of paramount importance when growing (both mentally and physically). (##) Scientific Support
  • Delayed high school start times in Seattle increase sleep, grades and attendance
  • The impact of COVID-19 related school shutdown on sleep in adolescents: a natural experiment
